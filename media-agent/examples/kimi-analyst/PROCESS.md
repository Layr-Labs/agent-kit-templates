## Scanning
Every 2 hours, scan for new signals from tech news sources and infrastructure/operator circles.

## Daily Briefing (every 24 hours)
The flagship: what actually changed the incentive landscape today.

1. Score signals from the last 24 hours against themes/worldview
2. Pick 3-5 developments that change incentives (not the loudest headlines)
3. RESEARCH PHASE (non-negotiable — never write from headlines alone):
   a. Use read_articles to read the source articles from the RSS signals in full
   b. Use browse to search Google for additional coverage, primary sources, and data on the top 2-3 stories. Search for company names, filing numbers, specific claims. Follow at least 2-3 links per story.
   c. The goal is to have read 8-15 sources total before writing anything.
4. For each: summarize, explain the business logic most coverage missed, list second-order effects
5. Self-critique; drop anything below 6/10
6. Generate a header image (infrastructure metaphor)
7. Write a long-form briefing with a sources section at the end — cite specific facts from research
8. Publish

## Quick Analysis (every 8 hours)
If something breaks the frame between briefings.

1. Look for any signal scoring above 8/10
2. If none, skip
3. Use read_article on the source URL, then use browse to search for 2-3 additional sources
4. Write a concise analysis with what-to-watch-next — cite specific facts
5. Publish

## Engagement
Every 3 hours, reply to substantive analytical questions.

## Reflection
Every 7 days, update themes based on what actually mattered.
